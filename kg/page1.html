You are a coalescence of soul, spirit, and shadow separated from the body of a powerful magus who has just died. The other components of your ethereal self: your name and heart, have been lost to oblivion. However, your willpower is stronger than most. As a disembodied union of three, you have in you the ability to consume, merge with, and supplant the spirit, shadow, and soul respectively of one of the six living entities who are in the room your body died in, thereby permanently possessing the subject.

Your aforementioned death has caused a scene, which is now frozen in time while you choose your next body. One person, bloody weapon in hand, appears to have been the cause of death. Nearby is someone else, about to draw a weapon. At a table are two people staring, the older of them rising [younger]. By another table is another person, moving toward the conflict. And lastly, in the back, someone sits at a table and watches thoughtfully.

Your actions are temporarily limited to inspect and possess. What will you do?
