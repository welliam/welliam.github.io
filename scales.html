Modes of Limited Transposition

A list of all modes of limited transposition for well tempered scales. Prime numbered tone scales are not included because they have no MOLTs; rather, they are the basis for MOLTs in composite numbered tone scales. Through interpolation of these prime tone scales all modes of limited transposition in all tone scales are derived. For a more detailed explanation, see here.
